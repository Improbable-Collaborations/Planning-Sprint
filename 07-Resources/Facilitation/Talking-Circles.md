# Talking Circles / Council Practice

**Indigenous traditions globally — First Nations, Māori, Andean**

---

Found across Indigenous traditions globally (First Nations, Māori, Andean). Governs turn-taking, deep listening, speaking from experience. The Ojai Foundation has been most prominent in formalising this in a cross-cultural context.

## H2GE Relevance

Pass the Leopard is essentially a talking circle with a Hitchhiker's twist. The principles of deep listening, speaking from experience, and governed turn-taking are foundational to how our events should work. The "you're not entitled to your opinion" value from H2G2 connects directly — speak from what you know, defer to those with direct experience.
