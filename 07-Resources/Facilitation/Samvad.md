# Samvad

**India — structured philosophical dialogue**

---

Structured philosophical dialogue with roots in the Upanishads. Practised in forms ranging from Brahmavihari to contemporary organisations like Antaragni.

## H2GE Relevance

The Upanishadic dialogue tradition — structured inquiry into fundamental questions through conversation — is a model for what our guides could become: not documents but living dialogues, archived and shared as wisdom literature rather than corporate reports.
