# Swaraj

**India — Gandhi's concept of self-rule at village scale**

---

Gandhi's concept of self-rule at village scale. Being revived by thinkers like Rajni Kothari and practitioners in the Eklavya network. Deeply commoning in spirit.

## H2GE Relevance

The principle of self-rule at the most local level, federated upward rather than delegated downward, is the philosophical foundation of our embassy/garden model. Swaraj is arguably the closest existing political philosophy to what Hitchhikers is attempting at planetary scale.
