# Sarvodaya Shramadana

**Sri Lanka — Buddhist-Gandhian tradition**

---

Buddhist-Gandhian community awakening model, working at village scale. Collective work camps as consciousness-raising. Genuinely bottom-up. A. T. Ariyaratne's framework is arguably one of the most sophisticated large-scale facilitation systems in existence, almost entirely unknown in Western facilitation circles.

## H2GE Relevance

The village-scale, bottom-up awakening model is a direct analogue to our embassy/garden concept. The collective work camp as consciousness-raising mirrors what we're attempting with six-week mission cycles. Worth deeper study.
