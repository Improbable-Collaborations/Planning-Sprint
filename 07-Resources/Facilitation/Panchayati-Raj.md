# Panchayati Raj / Gram Sabha

**India — ancient and constitutionally embedded**

---

Ancient and constitutionally embedded village council systems. Inherently participatory. The gap between principle and practice is vast, but the theory is sophisticated.

## H2GE Relevance

The constitutional embedding of local participatory governance is directly relevant to our constitutional drafting process. The Gram Sabha (village assembly) is a model for what local Hitchhiker assemblies could become — federated, autonomous, but part of a larger constitutional framework.
