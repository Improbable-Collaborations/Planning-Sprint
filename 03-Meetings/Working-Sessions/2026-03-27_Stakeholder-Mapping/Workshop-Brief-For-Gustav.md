## What We're Building Toward

The Hitchhiker's project needs three stakeholder maps. This session focuses on **the first**:

### 1. Community Map (this session)
A themed, prioritised map of communities that have energy and interest in participating over the next year. Who are they, how do they relate to each other, and which are essential vs. peripheral?

**Categories to explore:**
- Hitchhiker fan community (h2g2.com, regional groups — Australia, South America, etc.)
- Theatre companies and immersive event producers
- University departments (governance, constitution, political science, creative writing)
- Book towns and creative writing communities
- Regenerative agriculture / eco-village networks
- Tech communities (open source, privacy, decentralised systems)
- Constitutional / governance organisations
- Music and cultural festival networks
- International days and festivals (Comic-Con, Earth Day events, etc.)
- Partners already engaged: BFI, United Earth, LIONSBERG, OpenServ, LFG, KOTSA, Catalunya contacts

## Facilitation Notes

**Gustav's insight from the last call:** Different people in the project tell different stories about what it is. The most important thing is to surface those different perspectives and find where they align — "which train is everyone getting on, and which direction is it heading?"

**Practical consideration:** Some participants will be new to Gustav and to the broader project vision. A brief (10 min) recap of the first session's output and the project's current state will help ground everyone before diving in.

**Suggested structure:**
1. **Welcome and recap** (10 min) — Gustav shows the existing Mural board, brief context
2. **Perspectives round** (20 min) — each new participant shares: what communities do you know, what's your angle on this project?
3. **Mapping sprint** (40 min) — add new communities to the map, cluster by theme, indicate priority
4. **Relationships and gaps** (15 min) — draw connections, identify what's missing
5. **Prioritisation** (15 min) — top communities to engage first, next steps for each
6. **Wrap-up** (10 min) — capture outputs, agree on follow-up actions
