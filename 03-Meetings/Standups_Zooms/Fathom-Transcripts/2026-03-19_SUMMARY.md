## Waypoints — Magpie's Solar System Visualizer

- **What it is:** A persistent, seed-generated solar system visualizer — built in the last 10 days on ~$100 budget. Code didn't exist 10 days ago.
- **Hitchhiker lore layer:** All known locations from the books are mapped in — Frogstar System, GammaGran, Magrathea, etc. Fans can stake out neighborhoods early.
- **Persistent database:** Generate a solar system seeded from any name (e.g. "David Bovill") — that system persists. Could be used as data stores, agent homes, or VR spaces.
- **Social / cookie-clicker layer:** Players collect resources, build stations, connect with other players via code. The more connections, the more cosmetic space traffic in your system.
- **Milky Way MVP:** Currently a rough map of the Milky Way. Long-term vision: claimable real systems replace generated placeholders — people actually have a place in the universe.
- **Earth↔Galaxy transition:** The group flagged the value of moving seamlessly between a galaxy view and an Earth/local guide view (Google Earth → Google Maps style). Star Citizen already has similar tech (orbit to surface, no loading).
- **Malleable code:** Any piece can be extracted into a separate branch — the mission layer, the ships, the Earth overlay — independently of the rest.
- **Demo Friday:** Magpie agreed to present at Hitchhiker Theatre (same time Friday, Pacific) alongside other visual/storytelling work.

## Ahmed / Jade — Ardenova & NEOS OS

### Ardenova
A social media platform for *doing*:
- Users post **proposals** — identify a problem, break it into domain, cost, timeframe, sprint plan
- AI agents help generate the plan, pitch deck (via Gamma/Claude), and project structure
- Merges **crowdfunding + freelance platform** — no upfront cash needed to get started
- Once funded past a threshold → tasks assigned → contributors earn **project tokens** as a stake
- **Two-token model:** each project gets its own token; all tokens exchange against a platform token; platform token cashes out to fiat
- **Game SDK:** project tokens can be used in games/apps — keeps status competition in a digital realm, out of the real world
- Projects are time-scoped (sprint-based) — people sign up for tasks of defined hour-estimates and are rewarded accordingly
- ~90% built; final 10% is KYC, tokenization, and final plumbing

### NEOS Operating System (Josh Pasmore)
- Consent-based community operating system — "separation of church and state" from the economic layer
- Structure: **Ecosystems → Domains → Agreements → Proposals → Decisions**
- 54 agent skills for navigating the system
- AI agent is tapped into platform data — can answer "what agreements exist about X?" and assess compliance
- No impositions: participation is fully opt-in; people propose processes and others consent or don't
- Coaches onboard new members; the goal is for everyone to become a coach (defined by understanding of the system)
- Already live and public on GitHub

### Symbi (Time Banking layer)
- Ahmed is planning to tap into **Symbi** (symbiotic economy platform) for the time banking / mutual credit element
- Symbi already runs a time-banking mutual credit system; Ardenova would integrate rather than reinvent
- Key framing: "**access time**" — not just time-for-labor, but access to assets *via* time (tool libraries, spaces, etc.)

### 3Graphs1Identity (the core concept)

| Graph | What it is |
|---|---|
| **Knowledge Graph** | Memory and experience — can be as simple as a gardening club plant list or as complex as a human brain |
| **Promise Graph** | The lines between nodes — drawn by agents making and keeping promises. A promise degrades trust less than a broken imposition; a kept promise builds trust incrementally |
| **Trust Graph** | The emergent intersection of the other two — sovereign, personal, and grows more valuable over time |

- Agents can always see the full knowledge graph but can't traverse it without human choice (for now)
- The longer someone uses the system, the more unique and valuable their trajectory becomes
- **Proof of personhood without imposition:** instead of disclosing an iris scan (one node), you disclose the coordinates of your path through multiple knowledge graphs — far more unique, far more valuable when selectively disclosed

### Privacy Is Value Equation — v5
- Updated using the Braid research paper
- Inspired by the **Drake Equation** (multiplicative — any term going to zero collapses the whole)
- Three axes of separation Braid added: **agent / data / inference**
- Compression layers: experience (1:1) → memory (10:1) → knowledge (100:1) → understanding (100:1) → wisdom → reasoning graph (braid structure) → skill file (agent layer)

## Trust Graph + Hackathons / Time Bank (Mitchell's proposal)

> *"What if the thing that gets funded is the trust graph?"*

- Each hackathon participant = a node (filled with their project knowledge)
- Lines drawn by: (a) participants making promises to each other ("if your node is real, I promise to give you access to my identity system"), and (b) following through on those promises
- The resulting emergent trust graph = the judging mechanism
- Funding flows to the hackathon as a whole based on the density and integrity of its trust graph

**Applied to the Time Bank:**
- Goat herder posts node (10 goats, willing to host, will teach shepherding)
- Visitor promises to take care of goats → initial peer-to-peer verifiable relationship credential formed
- Promise fulfilled → both parties confirm → line hardens → **bilateral verifiable relationship credential** issued
- This is the first-person, peer-to-peer signal creation that forms proof of personhood organically
