Filter 2: Six-Week Workshop Cycles

How It Works

  • ~8 cycles per year (~352 days + holidays)
  • Each cycle: opening day → weekly rhythm → closing showcase
  • Closing events align with annual milestones
  • Content from one cycle feeds the next (podcasts, visuals, NFTs)
  • Globally coordinated: Africa → London → America

Podcast Schedule

  • Weekly release tied to cycle rhythm
  • Staggered by region? Or synchronised globally?
  • Feeds Hitchhiker Radio and social media calendar

Strengths

  • Sustained engagement, not one-off events
  • Built-in production schedule for content
  • Cross-pollination between regions

Considerations

  • Requires coordination infrastructure
  • Do cycles lead up to milestones or run independently?
  • Podcast cadence needs decision
  • How do 42 people in London connect meaningfully with 42 in Nairobi?
