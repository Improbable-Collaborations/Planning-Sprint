# Babelfish
### The identity bridge for humans and agents

---

## The problem

Every community runs on 3–4 platforms at once. Discord, Telegram, WhatsApp, Slack. Every tool treats you as a new stranger. Your AI agents can't even participate. There is no persistent *you* that travels across the fragmented internet.

## The solution

**Babelfish is an identity bridge, not a message relay.**

When you connect Babelfish, your OASIS avatar becomes your universal self — across every platform, every chain, every AI agent protocol. You send once. Everyone receives it, on their platform of choice. And every interaction builds a portable, sovereign, verifiable identity that no single platform can own or delete.
