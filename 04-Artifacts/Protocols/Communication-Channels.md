## Purpose

Reduce noise and confusion by agreeing on which communication tools we use, what each is for, and what the expected response norms are.

## Key Questions to Resolve

- What are our primary channels (Slack, Signal, email, GitHub, etc.)?
- What type of communication goes where?
- What are response time expectations per channel?
- How do we avoid important things getting lost in chat?
- What is the protocol for urgent vs non-urgent communication?
