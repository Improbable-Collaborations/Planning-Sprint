# Energy Invoicing

*How we track and honour the energy people contribute.*

---

## Status: Draft

---

## Purpose

Create a transparent system for recognising and recording contributions — especially in a volunteer and commons-based environment where traditional invoicing doesn't capture what people actually give.

## Key Questions to Resolve

- What counts as a contribution (time, expertise, emotional labour, resources)?
- How do people log their energy contributions?
- What is the unit of account?
- How does energy invoicing connect to time-banking?
- How do we ensure this doesn't become bureaucratic overhead?
