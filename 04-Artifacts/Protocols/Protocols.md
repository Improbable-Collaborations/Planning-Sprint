# Protocols

*Shared agreements for how we work together — so the machine runs smoothly and nobody has to read the sign on the leopard's door twice.*

---

Protocols are our operating agreements. They define how we coordinate, communicate, and hold each other accountable — not through hierarchy, but through clarity. Each protocol below is a living document: it starts simple, gets tested, and evolves as we learn.

These are not rules. They are commitments we make to each other so that energy flows where it's needed and nothing falls through the cracks.

## How to Use These

1. **Read the protocol** before you need it
2. **Follow it** until it doesn't work
3. **Flag it** when it needs updating (see [Flagging Issues](Flagging-Issues.md))
4. **Propose changes** — these are living documents, not laws carved in stone
