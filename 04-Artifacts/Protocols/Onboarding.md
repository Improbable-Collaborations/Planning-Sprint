## Purpose

Create a clear, welcoming path for new contributors so they can find their place and start adding value without needing to decode the whole system first.

## Key Questions to Resolve

- What does someone need to know on day one?
- Who is responsible for onboarding new people?
- What tools and access do they need?
- How do they find their first meaningful contribution?
- How do we match people to roles, projects, or working groups?
