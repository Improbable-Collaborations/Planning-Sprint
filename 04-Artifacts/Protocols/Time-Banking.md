*How we exchange time and skills as a reciprocal currency.*

## Status: Draft

## Purpose

Establish a time-banking system where contributors can exchange hours of work — one hour given equals one hour earned, regardless of the type of work. This creates a reciprocal economy within the project.

## Key Questions to Resolve

- What platform or system do we use to track time credits?
- Is one hour always equal to one hour, regardless of skill type?
- How do credits get earned and spent?
- Can credits be transferred between people?
- How does this relate to energy invoicing?
- What happens to unused credits?
