## Opening (5 min)
- Thank them for their time
- Explain purpose of the planning sprint
- Confirm how their input will be used

## Core Questions (20-30 min)

### Context & Role
1. What is your involvement with this initiative?
2. What does success look like from your perspective?

### Current State
3. What's working well today?
4. What are the biggest pain points or blockers?

### Priorities & Concerns
5. What are your top priorities for this effort?
6. What risks or concerns keep you up at night?
7. Are there any constraints we should be aware of?

### Relationships & Dependencies
8. Who else should we be talking to?
9. What teams or systems does this depend on?

### Vision
10. If we could only accomplish one thing, what should it be?

## Wrap-Up (5 min)
- Summarize key takeaways
- Ask if there's anything we missed
- Confirm follow-up steps
